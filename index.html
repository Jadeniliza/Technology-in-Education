COVID-19

Do you remember when all school were shut down almost everywhere in the world. Imagine if we didn't have technology. How would we have continued school? We would have been learning nothing for around a year. But thankfully, we have things like Zoom and Teams. We went on meetings with teachers and they could post assignments to do at home. It was still a flexible way of learning even in hard times.Now that we know that technology can help even during pandemics. How can we implement it when there are no pandemics? Like in daily life.

Technology in Daily Life

In KICS, Kigali International Community School, we use the internet in some way in the courses we take. In primary that used to be things to assess your knowledge like Kahoot and Quizizz but in secondary, they are much more focused around it. We have Computer Science/ Computer Skills classes, Google Classroom, and Savvas.Even though it's not all about technology it contains some aspects of it. And we should encourage other rural schools to start implementing this new way of learning.
