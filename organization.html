TechEd

What do we do?

Tech Ed is a non- profitable organization that works with children and teachers all over in Rwanda to ensure the best education for them with technology.

Our organization started in 2020 during the time of covid. What inspired me to start this organization was when I noticed that in so many rural areas in Rwanda. Kids were forced to go without education for a long time because of the fact that all schools were closing.While kids in urban areas could still continue their education with devices. On the local news, I saw that they started putting even classes that you could follow on TV for people who couldn't access computers or iPads. But I kept on thinking: what about the kids who don't have TVs at home? What are they doing now?
